About Us

Anahata yoga academy

Anahata Yoga Academy is a premier yoga studio nestled in Thoraipakkam, Chennai, dedicated to nurturing the mind, body, and spirit through the ancient practice of yoga. With highly experienced instructors like Dhilipan Sir and Aarty Ma'am, the academy offers a warm, welcoming environment that caters to students of all ages — from young children to adults seeking holistic wellness. Our approach blends traditional yoga philosophy with modern understanding to create sessions that are both transformative and deeply personal.

Boasting an outstanding rating of 4.9 out of 5, Anahata Yoga Academy has earned the trust and admiration of families and individuals across Chennai. Whether you are a beginner stepping onto the mat for the first time or an experienced practitioner looking to deepen your practice, our instructors bring positive energy, patience, and in-depth knowledge to every session. Join our growing community and discover the profound benefits of yoga — improved focus, inner calm, physical strength, and lasting well-being.

  • Rated 4.9/5 by 123 happy students — one of Chennai's most trusted yoga academies
  • Expert instructors experienced in teaching all age groups, from kids to seniors
  • Holistic approach combining traditional asanas, breathwork, and mindfulness for complete well-being
